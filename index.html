DA NANG

Da Nang is the 4th largest city in Vietnam after Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi and Hai Phong in terms of urbanization and socio-economic development. Located on the coast of the East Sea with the mouth of the Han River, Da Nang is one of the port cities with a strategic location in Central Vietnam and one of the five cities directly under the Central Government.

HOI AN

Traveling to Hoi An ancient town is an interesting and memorable experience for many domestic and foreign friends. Hoi An Ancient Town is a famous tourist destination about 30km southwest of Da Nang city center. Not noisy, bustling, Hoi An has a quiet and ancient beauty. The attraction of the 400-year-old urban area comes from ancient architectures, moss-roofed houses, colorful lantern streets, ...
